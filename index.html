About Me

Hi! My name is Sarah Jane, and I am a second-year student at Northeastern University. I’m a computer science major with a concentration in software, and I am also pursuing a minor in theatre. I am involved in several clubs on campus, primarily Generate and Origami Club. As part of Generate, I work on a 14-person team as a software engineer to create an application for a client. In Origami Club, I am the treasurer alongside an E-Board of five other members, where I manage the club's funds and contribute to leading meetings. I am also currently a teaching assistant for CS 2000: Introduction to Program Design and Implementation, where I help maintain the course material on the website for the course and tutor students one-on-one. Throughout my experiences, I have learned a lot about the computer science field and have become passionate about learning about all aspects of the field that I can. In particular, I’m most interested in back-end development, object-oriented design, and database management, especially with Java, Python, and SQL. I’d love to work in any position where I can contribute to a large-scale program, strengthening my current skills and gaining experience with new tools and frameworks.
